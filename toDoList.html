ToDoList

"A picture says more than a thousand words."
Fred R. Barnard

ToDoList is an android app that allows you to create tasks and notes with the addition to add images and photos to it, for improvement of memory.

Here you can simply check or delete single tasks or delet your whole list.

You can simply enter your task or item with the associated description, optionaly you'll be able to add an image or a photo.
