"A picture says more than a thousand words."

"Efforts were directed towards mastering Android app development within the framework of Android Studio."

In a team of 2, we developed an app in Android Studio, based on the detailed lessons and tools of the course. Basic concepts such as UI design, data management and background processes through fragments, broadcast receivers and asynchronous programming were integrated. The application uses Android features to store data and provides a user-friendly interface.

ToDoList is an android app that allows you to create tasks and notes with the addition to add images and photos to it, for improvement of memory.

Here you can simply check or delete single tasks or delet your whole list.

You can simply enter your task or item with the associated description, optionaly you'll be able to add an image or a photo.
